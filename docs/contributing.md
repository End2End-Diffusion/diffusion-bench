# Contributing

DiffusionBench is a community project. We want it to evolve into a comprehensive benchmark for diffusion transformer research. We invite the community to help build it.

## Ways to contribute

- **Add a new evaluation axis**: video, world models, editing.
- **Propose a new T2I metric** or stress-test SimpleEval.
- **Reproduce a published method** under DiffusionBench.
- **File issues** on metric drift, scoring bugs, or reproducibility gaps.

## How to join us

- **GitHub**: code, issues, pull requests at [End2End-Diffusion/diffusion-bench](https://github.com/End2End-Diffusion/diffusion-bench).
- **Discord**: chat, questions, coordination at our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/jh5Bz8uHEr).
